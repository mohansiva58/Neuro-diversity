Games & Activities

Fun, interactive games designed specifically for neurodivergent children to support learning and development.

Pattern Matching

Match patterns by color, shape, and size with increasingly challenging levels.

Ages 4-8 Visual Skills

Memory Tiles

Flip and match tiles to find pairs in this memory-enhancing game.

Ages 5+ Memory

Emotion Explorer

Learn to recognize facial expressions and match them to emotions.

Ages 3-7 Social Skills

Situation Station

Understand appropriate emotional responses to different situations.

Ages 6-10 Social Understanding

Creative digital art studio with calming sounds and visual effects.

Word Builder

Create words from letters with visual and audio support.

Story Maker

Pop virtual bubbles to practice precise movements and timing.

Drag and drop shapes into matching outlines with increasing difficulty.
